Crystal Collector!

You will be given a target number at the beggining of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each one has a random number assigned to it. Clicking the crystal will add that number to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score, you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the values of each crystal will be different.

Good luck and have fun!
